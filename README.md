# Pause Plate

A food craving intervention app that creates space between impulse and action.

## What It Does

Pause Plate helps you navigate food cravings with awareness instead of restriction. When a craving hits, the app guides you through a quick check-in process that interrupts automatic eating patterns and helps you make intentional choices.

### Core Features

- **Craving Logger** - Capture what you're craving, intensity level, and emotional context in seconds
- **Pause Timer** - Research-backed 10-minute delay that lets most cravings naturally subside
- **Pattern Insights** - Visualize your craving triggers across time, emotion, and situation
- **Substitution Suggestions** - Smart alternatives based on what you're actually craving (texture, flavor, emotional need)
- **Progress Tracking** - See how often you pause, how often cravings pass, and what works for you

## Why It Works

Most cravings peak and fade within 10-15 minutes. Pause Plate leverages this natural cycle by creating a structured pause that:

1. Breaks the craving-to-eating autopilot
2. Builds awareness of patterns and triggers  
3. Removes shame and restriction mentality
4. Empowers choice instead of control

This isn't about willpower or saying "no" to food. It's about creating the mental space to ask: *"Am I actually hungry, or is something else going on?"*

## Built For

People who struggle with emotional eating, mindless snacking, or feeling out of control around food. Whether you're stress-eating, boredom-snacking, or stuck in restrict-binge cycles—Pause Plate gives you a tool that's supportive, not punitive.
